SMS Terms & Conditions

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Program Description

HomeShield Pros offers optional SMS/text message communications to homeowners who submit service requests and provide explicit SMS consent via our website. Messages include service request updates, appointment scheduling information, proposal details, and local home care alerts related to weather events or air quality concerns in your area.

SMS consent is entirely optional and is never required to request a service or receive a proposal from HomeShield Pros.

How You Opt In

You opt in to SMS communications by checking the optional SMS consent checkbox on our service request form at homeshieldpronow.com. The checkbox is unchecked by default and clearly labeled as optional. By checking this box, you provide express written consent to receive text messages from HomeShield Pros at the mobile number you provided.

Message Frequency

Message frequency may vary. You may receive messages related to your active service request, appointment scheduling, proposal updates, and local home care alerts triggered by weather events or air quality concerns in your zip code. We do not send unrelated promotional blasts to opted-in contacts.

Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Please check with your mobile carrier for details about your messaging plan and any applicable charges.

How to Opt Out

Reply STOP to any message to immediately cancel all SMS communications from HomeShield Pros.

After replying STOP, you will receive a single confirmation message and no further SMS messages will be sent. You may re-subscribe at any time by texting START to the same number or by re-submitting your opt-in via our website form.

How to Get Help

Reply HELP to any SMS message for assistance. You may also contact us directly at:

  • Email: info@homeshieldpronow.com
  • Website: homeshieldpronow.com

Supported Carriers

SMS service is available on most major US carriers including AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, and others. Carrier support is not guaranteed on all networks.

Privacy

Your mobile number and SMS consent status are not sold or shared with third parties for their own marketing purposes. Your information is used only to deliver communications related to your HomeShield Pros service request and local home care alerts. See our full Privacy Policy for complete details on how we handle your information.

Changes to SMS Terms

We reserve the right to update these SMS Terms at any time. Updated terms will be posted at this URL with a revised date. Continued use of SMS communications after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.
